How to Play:
🩷 Player 1 (Pink cups, left side) moves right →
💙 Player 2 (Blue cups, right side) moves left ←

Rules:
• A cup can slide into an adjacent empty cell
• A cup can jump over exactly one opposing cup into an empty cell
• You cannot jump over 2+ cups or your own cups
• Only one move per turn
• First to get all 3 cups to the other side wins!
